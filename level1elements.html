Demonstrate Understanding of the Elements of Dance

This standard requires the student to complete a series of activities that demonstrate the student's understanding of the elements of dance. You could do this with a practical demonstration, posters or images, bullet point notes, etc. Complete the activities with a partner or group and keep a record of your contribution. All activities will look at body, space, time, energy, relationships.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved the student must complete at least five activities and identify and explore a range of dance elements.

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit the student must complete at least five activities and identify and explore a range of dance elements. The student must also explore uses of the elements and apply them in contexts and reflect on the use of the element.

Techniques for Excelence

To gain Excellence the student must complete at least five activities and identify and explore a range of dance elements. The student must explore the range of uses of the elements and apply them to a range of contexts and evaluate their effect/purpose.
