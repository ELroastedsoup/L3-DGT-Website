Perform a Theatre Dance to Communicate Understanding of the Dance

This standard requires the student to learn and perform a theatre dance. You will be assessed on how clear your individual performance is.

Techniques for Achieved

>To gain achieved the student must perform a group theatre dance of at least 2 and up to 4 minutes of tap.

  • Recalls and reproduces the group theatre dance

  • Uses appropriate focus for the dance

  • The dance must be performed for an audience

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit the student must perform a group theatre dance of at least 2 and up to 4 minutes of tap.

  • Recalls and reproduces the group theatre dance li>
  • Uses appropriate and consistent focus for the dance

  • The dance must be performed for an audience
