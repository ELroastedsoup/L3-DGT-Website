Perform a Repertoire of Contrasting Dances

In this standard it is required that the student perform three contrasting dances (Tap, Hip-Hop, Jazz.) The dances must be 2-3 minutes long and be reassessed throughout the year.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved it is required that the student perform a repertoire of three contrasting dances. You may consider:

  • Reproducing correct movement for all three dances

  • Generally accurate timing

  • Conveys different ideas in all three dances

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit it is required that the student perform a repertoire of three contrasting dances with understanding of the stylistic requirements. You may consider:

  • Reproduces correct movement for all three dances with appropriate posture

  • Accurate timing in each three dances

  • Performs with clear focus (expressions)

  • Conveys different ideas in all three dances

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence it is required that the student perform a repertoire of three contrasting dances with in-depth understanding of the stylistic requirements. You may consider:

  • Reproducing correct movement for all three dances with clarity and contrasting details

  • Demonstrates use of varied dynamics and flow

  • Conveys the emotional content and expressions of each dance
