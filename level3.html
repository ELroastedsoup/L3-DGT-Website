Level 3 dance at KingsWay school follows the NCEA curriculum. There are four standards in the NCEA level 2 curriculum, perform a solo or duet, perform a repertoire of contrasting dances, perform a group dance, choreograph a dance to develop and resolve ideas. On this page you will find information into each standard and what is required to gain achieved, merit and excellence.
