Perform a Group Dance

In this standard it is required that the student performs a 2-minute-long contemporary dance. You will be assessed on your technique, focus, expression, dynamics, projection, ensemble awareness.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved it is required that the student performs in a group dance. You may consider:

  • Technique (contemporary arms, parallels, timing, space, posture, etc.)
  • Focus remains in the performance.
  • Dynamics (use of energy, contrasts)
  • Ensemble (eye contact, focus on hands, timing with fellow dancers.)

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit it is required that the student perform in a group dance competently. You may consider:

  • Control of technique (confident arms and footwork, spatial awareness, transitions, contrasts.)
  • Interpretation of dynamics (graceful to music, energy, purpose.)
  • Ensemble (aware of partnership, welcoming expression, consistent eye contact, sustained connection with audience.)

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence it is required that the student performs in a group dance skillfully. You may consider:

  • Adapt execution of technique (confident clear movements and placements, fluidity, precise movement, timing, space, posture.)
  • Interpretation of expressive aspects of the dance (emphasis, dynamics, expressions, etc.)
  • Ensemble (meaningful interaction between dancers, dancer creates a character, eye contact.)
  • Connection to partner and audience
