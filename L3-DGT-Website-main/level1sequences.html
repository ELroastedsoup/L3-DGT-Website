Perform Dance Sequences

The student will perform three dance sequences from different genres. Each sequence must be at least 45 seconds long.

Sequence 1: Jazz. The Jazz sequence will be dynamic and sharp, you will be assessed on your jazz technique (turns, jumps, etc.)

Sequence 2: Lyrical. The lyrical dance will be emotional. You will be assessed on your ability to perform smooth and sharp contrasts with the music as well as control and clarity.

Sequence 3: Tap. The tap dance will be focused on your percussion. You will be assessed on your ability to perform clarity of beats as well as musicality (facials, energy shifts, dynamics.)

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved you will need to:

  • Movements, gestures, shapes, and actions may lack clarity
  • Still shapes at the start and beginning of dance
  • Technique for jazz, lyrical, and tap may lack full execution
  • Eye focus mostly on audience
  • Facials and energy may lack

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit you will need to:

  • Movements, gestures, shapes, and actions are beginning to develop clarity and precision
  • Technique for jazz, lyrical and tap is executed correctly but still needs improvement
  • Strong energy and facials are evident
  • Shows confidence in the routine
  • Shapes are starting to clearly show

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence you will need to:

  • Movements, gestures, shapes, and actions are continually clear and precise
  • Technique for jazz, lyrical and tap is excellently executed
  • Movements are strong and dynamics are evident
  • Energy and facials are constantly apparent
  • Confident throughout the dance
