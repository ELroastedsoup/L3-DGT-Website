Perform a Solo or Duet

In this standard it is required that a student learn and perform a lyrical duet. You will be marked on your technique, dynamics, sensitivity with partner.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved the student must perform a duet. You should consider:

  • Generally, appropriate Technique (reproduction of movements, shapes, timing, etc.)
  • Maintained focus (focus on partner/audience.)
  • Facial expression
  • Dynamics (energy changes)

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit the student must perform a duet competently. You should consider:

  • Controlled technique (reproduction of movements, shapes, timing, etc.)
  • Maintained focus (focus on partner and audience, focus that supports communication of dance.)
  • Facial expression
  • Dynamics (energy changes)

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence the student must perform a duet skillfully. You should consider:

  • Adept execution of technique (reproduction of movements, shapes, timing, etc.)
  • Focus (focus on partner and audience, focus that supports communication of dance.)
  • Projection and connection with the audience
  • Dynamics (energy changes)
