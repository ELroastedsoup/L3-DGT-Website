Level 1 dance at KingsWay school does not follow the NCEA curriculum but its own curriculum. There are 4 standards in this curriculum, compose dance sequences for given briefs, perform dance sequences, demonstrate ensemble skills in a dance, and lastly, demonstrate understanding of the elements of dance. On this page you will find information into each standard and what is required to gain achieved, merit and excellence.
