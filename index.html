About KingsWay School Dance

Kingsway school dance is an amazing curriculum where students are welcomed to express and perform their emotions through dance. KingsWay school follows NCEA dance guidelines for level 2 and 3 but for level 1 KingsWay uses their own curriculum. NCEA describes dance as “Dance is about learning to communicate through movement and interpret meaning from movement.” In dance we learn and choreograph dances which are performed in the KingsWay schools annual Arts Week. This creates an amazing opportunity for students to work towards something and create a healthy learning environment. On this website there is all the information needed about each standard in level 1, 2 and 3. Navigate the website by using the hamburger menu on the top right of the screen.
