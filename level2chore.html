Choreograph a Group Dance to Communicate an Intention

In this standard it is required that in groups of 3 or more you choreograph a 2-minute dance. The dance must be based off images of humanity. Each group will choose a image and a quote to create their dance about.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved it is required that the student has:

  • Defined a clear intention in a written statement
  • Developed movement which relates to their chosen idea
  • Chosen specific movements from the material and developed and ordered the movements
  • Used spatial relationships among the dancers with formations, facings, and groupings

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit it is required that the student has:

  • Defined a clear intention in a written statement
  • Developed effective movement relating to the chosen idea
  • Chosen specific movements from the material and developed and ordered the movements
  • Made choices about the use of spatial relationships with formations, facings, and groupings

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence it is required that the student has:

  • Defined a clear intention in a written statement
  • Developed and selected unusual movement relating to their chosen idea
  • Chosen specific movements from the material and developed and ordered the movements
  • Made choices about the use of spatial relationships with formations, facings, and groupings
  • Made choices about the use of spatial relationships with formations, facings, and groupings
