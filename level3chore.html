Choreograph a Dance to Develop and Resolve Ideas

This standard requires the student to develop ideas based on their quote “Our lives begin to end the day we become silent about things that matter.” You will discuss a silenced issue. You will need three main sections, section one: establishment, section two: development, and section three: resolve. The dance will be at least 2 minutes long.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved it is required that the student choreograph a group dance to develop and resolve ideas. To do this it is required that the student choreographs a dance that communicates an idea based on the quote. You may consider:

  • Interpreted the quote

  • Manipulated dance elements such as shapes, space, level, pathways, groupings, etc.

  • A structural cannon has been used to create a ‘domino’ effect to increase and decrease the tension.

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit it is required that the student choreograph a group dance to effectively develop and resolve ideas. The student must create a dance that communicates an effectively developed concept. You may consider:

  • Summarized ideas from quote

  • Created a dance that effectively demonstrates refined ideas (weight sharing moments, transitions, relationships.)

  • Created a dance that effectively orders choreographic components (speed and duration of movement.)

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence it is required that the student choreograph a group dance to fully develop and resolve ideas. To do this the student has created a dance that communicates a fully developed concept. You may consider:

  • Created a dance that demonstrates developed ideas (tension, climax, sequencing of movement, clearly demonstrated structure of ideas.)

  • Creates a dance that synthesizes all choreographic components (unity, shapes, levels, relationships, etc.)
