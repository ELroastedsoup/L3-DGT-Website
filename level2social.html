Perform an Ethnic or Social Dance to Communicate Understanding of the Style

The standard requires the student to perform a social dance (Hip-Hop). You will be assessed on how clearly your performance demonstrates your understanding of the genre.

Techniques for Achieved

To gain achieved the student will need to perform a 1–2-minute Hip-Hop dance that communicates the understanding of the style. You may consider:

  • Appropriate focus on incorporating elements of Hip-Hop culture

  • Energy

  • Attitude

Techniques for Merit

To gain merit the student will need to perform a 1–2-minute Hip-Hop dance that communicates an in depth understanding of the style. You may consider:

  • Accurate Hip-Hop movements (shapes, posture, timing.)

  • Consistent focus on energy, attitude, and personal expression

Techniques for Excelence

To gain excellence the student will need to perform a 1–2-minute Hip-Hop dance that communicates a comprehensive understanding of the style. You may consider:

  • Accurate and appropriate clarity, control, shapes, posture, movements, and timing

  • Consistent energy and performance with added attitude and personal expression
